Leveraging Machine Learning in the Design of Novel Ionic Liquids

Skills: Python · Data Analysis · Data Visualization · Institutional Research

During my NSF-REU research internship, I had the opportunity to delve into the exciting field of machine learning to design novel ionic liquids. I played a crucial role in implementing and optimizing a Python-based codebase for data analysis, which significantly improved the efficiency and accuracy of property predictions. One of my major tasks involved comprehensive data preprocessing and cleaning for over 100k data points. Utilizing Python packages such as NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Scikit-learn, I was able to process and visualize complex data effectively. I also had the privilege of presenting my findings through presentations, a poster, and a research paper, further refining my scientific communication skills.
